About Highbramble Park

Highbramble Park is a world-class Standardbred training center located in the heart of Kentucky. Our state-of-the-art facility is designed to cater to the needs of both professional horsemen and the general public.

With capacity for up to 700 horses, our center offers top-notch training facilities, a gourmet restaurant, and a picturesque lake for recreation and water supply.
